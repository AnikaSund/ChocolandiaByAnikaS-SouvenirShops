Souvenir Shops
Chocolandia features many souvenir shops. To be exact, 11. Many are like one another, but most are unique. Here are some details about these souvenir shops.


Water Ride Souvenir Shops
There are 3 specified water ride souvenir shops, but other souvenir shops include some water wear and care supplies. The first water souvenir shop is located in the center of the park, along with a few other souvenir shops and the food court. The second water souvenir shop is located near Raging Rock Candy, and the third water souvenir shop is near Laffy Taffy Logs. These souvenir shops include items such as slippers, swimwear, goggles, swim caps, clothing items, and other accessories. The clothing items and accessories are swim related. For example, one shirt says "I survived the Raging Rock Candy Water Ride at Chocolandia". Make sure to visit the water ride souvenir shops.
Laffy Taffy Logs


Clothing Souvenir Shops
Chocolandia clothing items are sold in mostly every souvenir shop, but there are 5 specified clothing souvenir shops. The first 2 are in the center of the park, near the food court and the stage. The third one is near the ride, Lollipop Swirls. The fourth one is near the entrance to the park, and the fifth one is located near the rides Reese's recess, Gingerbread House of Horrors, and the Marshmellow Flight. These shops include all Chocolandia merch, from dresses to socks. Be sure to check out the clothing souvenir shops!
Gingerbread House of Horrors


Other Souvenir Shops
There are 3 other, non classified souvenir shops in Chocolandia. The first one sells mostly jewelry and a couple of hats and other souvenir items. This souvenir shop is located near Mint Mayhem. The last ones sell anything from mugs to magnets. These are located in the center of the park, near the food court and the ride Spinning Sour Patches. If you're looking for a mini, inexpensive souvenir, these are the souvenir shops to go to!
